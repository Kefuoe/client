People centred mining modernization

It is imperative to prevent and minimise occurances of mining accidents, to ensure continued mineral supply and no job losses. For this reason, integrating modernised mining methods can significantly prolong employment periods and ensure a persistent survival of a lot of households in South Africa. The future of modernisation aims to train and equip workers from all demographics with all necessary skills for genrations to come.
